Bootcamp

We teach our members core data science skills through a bootcamp that takes our members on a start-to-finish journey, touching on essentials such as cleaning data to more advanced concepts, such as feature engineering. Workshops are project-based and all members leave bootcamp with a fully polished, professional data science project.

Learn more at our bootcamp site!

Hacklytics

We organize a yearly datathon, Hacklytics, for those of all skill levels to hone in on their data science skills. We also assist chapters and individuals in running their own data science projects and datathons

Projects

DSGT arranges projects with corporate sponsors to allow members to work on real life data science problems all while getting paid. We also organize company events and talks that allow our members to build a network and learn more about the field of data science. Below are our ongoing projects:

Stormalytics
The National Weather Service uses outdated, stats-based models to try to predict tornadoes. Using tornado records, and the available rich doppler data, DSGT is trying to build an intelligent tornado activity prediction system. We hope to be able to predict tornadoes earlier than the current standard, saving lives in the process.

Mentra
The Mentra Team is currently working on developing a Natural Language Processing (NLP) classifier that can group any given job description into a group of skills that are necessary to succeed in that job. This will help the start-up Mentra in their goal to design an employment-matching system for individuals on the autism spectrum.
